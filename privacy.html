WAKE — Privacy Policy

Effective 8 July 2026. Developer: Game'n. Contact: info.wake.game@gmail.com.

WAKE is a single-player arcade game. This policy explains, in plain terms, what the app handles and what it does not. The short version: the game keeps your progress on your own device, it shows ads through Google AdMob, and it offers one optional purchase through Google Play. There are no accounts, and we do not run a server that stores information about you.

Information stored on your device

Your game state — your score, your settings, and the graveyard of past runs — is saved locally on your device only. It is not transmitted to us or to anyone else. You can erase it at any time by clearing the app's storage or uninstalling the app.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

The free version of the game shows ads served by Google AdMob. To deliver and measure ads, Google and its ad partners may collect and process device information, including a device advertising identifier, coarse (non-precise) location inferred from your IP address, and app-interaction and diagnostic data. We do not receive this data ourselves; it is handled by Google. Google's handling is governed by the Google Privacy & Terms.

In the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, and Switzerland, the app presents a consent choice (via Google's User Messaging Platform) before ads load. If you decline personalised ads, you may still see non-personalised ads. You can change your choice at any time from Settings → Privacy & ads → Ad privacy choices inside the game. You can also reset or limit your advertising identifier in your device's system settings.

Purchases

The game offers a single optional one-time purchase that removes ads and unlocks extra features. Payment is processed by Google Play. We receive confirmation of whether the purchase was made; we do not receive or store your payment card or billing details.

Children

WAKE is not directed to children. Because it contains ads, it is not part of any "designed for families" programme. If you believe a child has used the app, clearing the app's storage removes all locally saved data.

How information is shared

We do not sell your data. The only sharing that occurs is between you and Google (and Google's ad partners) for the purpose of serving ads and processing your optional purchase, as described above. This sharing happens through Google's own SDKs, under Google's policies.

Data retention

We hold no data about you on any server. Game data lives on your device until you clear it or uninstall. Data processed by Google for ads is retained according to Google's policies.

Your rights

Because we do not store personal information about you, there is nothing on our side to access, correct, or delete; clearing the app's storage removes everything the app keeps. For data processed by Google for advertising, you can exercise choices through the in-app ad privacy controls, your device advertising settings, and Google's own tools. If you have a question, contact us at info.wake.game@gmail.com.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this page with a new effective date.
